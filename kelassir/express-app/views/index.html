Gender-Based Violence Awareness

Gender-Based Violence (GBV) refers to harmful acts directed at individuals based on their gender. It includes physical, sexual, psychological, and economic abuse.

Why It Matters in Malaysia

In Malaysia, GBV is a growing concern. Since the COVID-19 pandemic, domestic violence cases have surged. Cultural norms and systemic inequality contribute to the issue.

Where to Get Help

  • Talian Kasih 15999 – 24/7 helpline
  • One-Stop Crisis Centres (OSCC) – Government hospitals
  • WAO – wao.org.my
